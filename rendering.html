Rendering Expertise

Premium finishes delivered by accredited specialists.

KYR Construction is a rendering-led contractor with 15+ years of experience delivering high-performance exteriors across residential, commercial, new-build, and refurbishment projects.

As approved applicators of K Rend and Weber systems, we follow manufacturer guidance precisely, ensuring consistent finish quality, moisture control, and long-term durability.

We support clients from specification through to final inspection, offering sample panels, colour guidance, and site sequencing advice.

Render Systems

Tailored systems for every exterior requirement.

K Rend Silicone Render

Highly durable silicone render with self-cleaning properties and exceptional weather resistance. Ideal for contemporary exteriors requiring low maintenance and long-term colour stability.

Best suited for exposed elevations and high-traffic residential developments.

K Rend Monocouche

Single-coat render providing efficient application with a robust, textured finish. Consistent colour and grain make it ideal for large exteriors and new-build housing.

A cost-effective option that delivers strong performance and clean detailing.

Weber Render Systems

Proven Weber systems that combine flexibility with weather resilience. We specify the right Weber solution for the building type, ensuring controlled moisture management and consistent finishes.

Ideal for mixed-use developments and refurbishment schemes requiring dependable performance.

Insulated Render / EWI Systems

External wall insulation systems that improve thermal performance while delivering a premium finish. We manage insulation boards, fixings, mesh, and top coat for a complete system.

Ideal for energy upgrades and retrofit schemes.

Acrylic & Mineral Renders

Versatile acrylic and mineral render options offering a broad range of textures and colours. These systems provide flexibility for architectural detailing and heritage-sensitive projects.

Best used where breathable finishes or specific textures are required.
